About us

Government Boys Senior Secondary School - Rajokari, Rajokri, New Delhi, is a senior secondary school recognised and affiliated to CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education).We believe in developing young minds to flourish and thrive. Our school is a lively and energetic learning community, dedicated to allowing with knowledge and values that will guide them towards a bright future.

Established in 1970, Government Boys Senior Secondary School - Rajokari, operating under the National Board - Central Board of Secondary Education, is a government-funded institution committed to providing quality education to low-income students. Our school, administered by the South West A District’s Directorate of Education and supported by central and state government funding, offers not only a rigorous curriculum but also free lunch, financial aid for clothing and notebooks, and free curriculum books to ensure that financial barriers do not hinder our students' access to education.

We have  experienced and passionate teachers, who are committed to inspire students. Through innovative teaching methods and modern resources, we aim to equip our students with essential skills and a strong moral compass.
