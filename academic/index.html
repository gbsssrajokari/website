instructions for parents

daily check

You are humbly requested to check your ward’s haircut, uniform, and punctuality every morning before sending them to school.

Communication & Guidance

Feel free to write to the school about any concerns or seek guidance for your ward. Your concerns willbe highly respected and responded with utmost priority

Time Table & Books

Ensure your ward carries the required books and exercise books according to the days Time Table. Avoid carrying extra books

Balancing Activities

Encourage your ward to strike a balance between studies, co-curricular activities, and sports. Also, advise them to manage daily pocket money responsibly and avoid excessive spending.

Almanac Monitoring

Check your ward’s almanac daily and sign any remarks if present. Take necessary action based on the remarks

instructions for students

Hair Guidelines

Girls with hair below the shoulders should plait it, while short hair should be neatly cut and pinned with black pins.

Respect & Responsibility

Students must respect others’ property, including school belongings. Any damage, even accidental, should be reported immediately. The school is not responsible for lost valuables, so it is advisable not to bring them to school.

Almanac & Identity

Please check your ward’s haircut, uniform, and punctuality every morning before sending them to school.

Transport Rules

Students using their own transport should arrive before the bell rings. Self-driven scooters, motorcycles, or cars are not allowed for commuting to school

Dress Code

Neat and proper school uniform should be worn on all working days, functions, and excursions. Non-Sikh boys should maintain regular haircuts.

About Us

Government Boys Senior Secondary School - Rajokari, Rajokri, New Delhi, is a senior secondary school recognised and affiliated to CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education).
